Recipe Tracking App - CookMate**

**Overview**
CookMate will be a simple yet effective recipe tracking app for home cooks who wish to arrange, 
personalise, and revisit their favourite recipes. CookMate gives users the ability to enter and oversee
their own culinary creations, in contrast to commercial cooking platforms that emphasise on 
professionally produced content. CookMate makes recipe management intimate and stress-free, 
whether you're recording a family favourite, modifying ingredients to suit dietary requirements, or 
just documenting successful culinary experiments.

**Problem Statement**
Many home cooks love trying out new recipes or creating their own, but they often struggle to keep 
track of what they have cooked, what changes they made, or which dishes were a success. Most 
existing cooking apps focus on providing professional recipes, not helping users organize their 
personal cooking history. There isalso a need for an easy way to filter recipes based on dietary 
needs and quickly share them with friends or family. This creates an opportunity for a personalized 
recipe tracking app that lets users log, customize, and manage their cooking experiences in one 
simple, user-friendly space.

**Why CookMate**
I enjoy cooking and often try out new recipes or create my own variations. However, I usually 
forget the exact ingredients or steps I changed, which makes it hard to recreate successful dishes. 
This scenario is meaningful to me because I have experienced the problem firsthand. Creating an 
app like CookMate would help not only me but also others who want a simple way to keep track of 
their personal recipes, especially when cooking for different dietary needs. It combines my interest 
in food with my passion for solving real-world problems through technology.
Target Audience
• Home cooks
• Food enthusiasts
• People with dietary restrictions (e.g., vegan, gluten-free)
• Individuals who want to organize their personal or shared recipes


**Relaed Apps**
App 1: Tasty
Platform: Android/iOS
Link: Tasty App
Strengths: Beautiful interface, step-by-step videos, community recipes
Weaknesses: No personal recipe tracking or customization options
How My App Is Different: My app will focus on personal recipe tracking and allow users to 
customize, review, and add their own creations.
App 2: Paprika Recipe Manager
Platform: Android/iOS
Link: Paprika App
Strengths: Great for storing recipes, grocery lists, and meal planning
Weaknesses: Paid app, complex UI for casual users
How My App Is Different: Free, Simple UI and advanced dietary filters
App 3: Yummly 
Platform: Android/iOS 
Link: Yummly App
Strengths: Personalized recommendations, meal planning 
Weaknesses: Limited ability to add custom recipes and track change 
How My App Is Different: Offline recipe storage, a Minimal user interface, and simpler tracking 
history
Features
1. User Features
• Sign in /sign up
• Add/edit/delete personal recipes
• Save favourite recipes
2. Core Features
• Input recipe details (title, ingredients, steps, cook time)
• Add notes or modifications
• Search and Filters Recipes (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
3. UI Features
• Tabbed bottom navigation: Home, Add Recipe, Favourites, Profile
• Recipe cards with photo, title, and tags
• Recipe input form
