# 🍳 CookMate - Recipe Tracking App

## Overview

**CookMate** is a simple yet effective recipe tracking app designed for home cooks who want to organize, personalize, and revisit their favorite culinary creations. Unlike commercial platforms that focus on professionally curated recipes, CookMate allows users to manage their own cooking experiences—whether it's logging a family favorite, tweaking ingredients for dietary needs, or recording experimental dishes that turned out great.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Many home cooks experiment with recipes or invent their own dishes but struggle to:

* Track what they've cooked
* Remember what changes they made
* Recreate successful meals
* Share custom recipes with friends or family
* Filter dishes based on dietary restrictions

Most existing apps offer limited support for personal recipe logging. **CookMate** fills this gap by providing a simple, intuitive space for managing and revisiting cooking experiences.

## 💡 Why CookMate?

As a passionate cook, I often forget the small adjustments I make while trying new recipes. Recreating those dishes becomes difficult without a proper log. **CookMate** solves this by letting users:

* Log personal recipes and variations
* Tag recipes for dietary needs
* Maintain a cooking history
* Share recipes with others

This project merges my love for cooking and problem-solving through technology.

## 👥 Target Audience

* Home cooks
* Food enthusiasts
* Individuals with dietary restrictions (e.g., vegan, gluten-free)
* People looking to organize personal/shared recipes

## 🔧 Features

### 🧑‍🍳 User Features

* Sign in / Sign up
* Add / Edit / Delete personal recipes
* Save favorite recipes

### 🧠 Core Features

* Input recipe details: title, ingredients, steps, cook time
* Add personal notes or modifications
* Search & filter recipes (e.g., vegan, gluten-free)

### 🎨 UI Features

* Tabbed bottom navigation: Home, Add Recipe, Favourites, Profile
* Recipe cards with photo, title, and tags
* User-friendly recipe input form
